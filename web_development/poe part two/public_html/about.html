Our Journey: Chic Beauty

Chic Beauty was born from a passion for empowering individuals through high-quality, ethical beauty. Designed, curated, and assembled in **Johannesburg, South Africa**, we began our journey in **2018**. Our vision is to **change the world** with products that break traditional beauty norms and celebrate diverse expressions.

We believe that true beauty encompasses not just what you apply, but also how it's made. This guides our commitment to **cruelty-free ingredients, ethical marketing, and sustainable practices** in every step of our creation process.

Who We're For

Our collections are crafted for **teens and young adults** seeking trendy, effective, and affordable beauty products. We resonate with those who value authenticity, self-expression, and a conscious approach to beauty.

We foster a vibrant community, leveraging engaging content and collaborations with influencers to inspire and connect with our audience.

Milestones in Beauty

2018
Chic Beauty (formerly Chick Cosmetics) was founded by Nomfundo in Johannesburg, South Africa, with a vision for innovative beauty.
2019
Launched our first range of highly successful, cruelty-free eyeshadow palettes.
2021
Expanded into sustainable skincare, focusing on natural and effective ingredients.
Today
Continue to innovate with new collections, committed to ethical practices and community engagement.
